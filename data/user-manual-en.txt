                0. Introduction





    Thank you for choosing the VEILORA secure smartphone. In today's highly digital world, threats such as data breaches, network eavesdropping, and privacy tracking are omnipresent. VEILORA is not just an ordinary smart device; it is a "mobile digital fortress" meticulously crafted for you.
    VEILORA is a professional operating system dedicated to digital privacy protection and cybersecurity. It is designed for military personnel, law enforcement officers, government officials, high-value business professionals, scientists, and other individuals who require an exceptionally high level of digital security protection. Powered by a proprietary security architecture, VEILORA integrates eight core capabilities: cybersecurity, protection, privacy management, isolated storage, encrypted communications, emergency protection, anti-extraction safeguards, secure cloud backup, and intelligent threat detection. Together, these capabilities establish a comprehensive security framework that protects data throughout its entire lifecycle— from collection and transmission to storage and use—helping safeguard endpoint devices and sensitive information at the system level.


            0.1 About This Manual


    This manual is designed to help you quickly understand and master the exclusive security features and daily operational guidelines of your VEILORA smartphone. Because this device incorporates unique low-level encryption and isolation mechanisms, we strongly recommend that you carefully read all sections of this manual before your first use, even if you are highly familiar with standard smartphones.


            0.2 Safety and Compliance Statement


    Before enjoying the ultimate secure experience brought by VEILORA, please carefully read and acknowledge the following statements and compliance terms:
1. Disclaimer: Your core security keys (such as the account recovery phrase, Private Space password, and device reset password) are exclusively stored locally in the device's hardware security module and must be kept offline by you. Our official servers absolutely do not back up or store any of your decryption credentials. If you


lose your recovery phrase or forget your password, resulting in the inability to decrypt data or a permanent device lockout, VEILORA and its authorized service providers cannot offer forced unlocking or data recovery services, nor will we bear any joint liability for any direct or indirect data loss arising from such situations.
2. Security Usage Responsibilities: The advanced security features provided by this device, such as Encrypted Instant Messaging, One-Tap Recovery, and Network Stealth, are designed to protect users' lawful communication privacy and legitimate digital assets. As the sole owner of the device, you must properly safeguard the device and its security credentials, and assume all legal consequences arising from its use. It is strictly prohibited to use this device for any illegal activities that violate the laws and regulations of your local country or region.
3. System Integrity Statement: Do not attempt any unauthorized privilege escalation (Rooting), bootloader unlocking, or flashing of third-party firmware on this device. Such high-risk operations will trigger an irreversible device lockout or complete data wipe, and will immediately void your warranty.

                2.  How to Create and Set Up a Private Space




            2.1  System-Wide Multi-Level Isolated Encrypted Storage


    Physical-Level Multi-Space Isolation: Each space features an independent UID, independently running apps, separate storage partitions, and independent encryption keys. They remain completely isolated and do not interfere with each other.
    App Sandbox Isolation: Applications are strictly isolated from one another, significantly mitigating the risk of malicious data theft and data leaks between apps.
    File-Level Fine-Grained Encryption: Different users and storage spaces are equipped with distinct encryption keys, achieving precise cryptographic protection down to the individual file and space level.

                4. How to Prevent App Privacy Theft





    Breaking through traditional static permission authorization models, VEILORA constructs a dynamic privacy protection architecture featuring "Real-Time Auditing, Full-Process Monitoring, Proactive Interception, and One-Tap Protection." This enhances the endpoint's ability to manage privacy permissions across multiple scenarios and states, effectively mitigating security risks such as malicious permission acquisition, background data theft, and unauthorized access by applications.


            4.1  App Permission Control and Risk Auditing


    The system actively monitors the OS and all third-party apps in real-time— whether running in the foreground, suspended in the background, or during screen lock standby. It tracks access requests and operations involving privacy resources such as contacts, albums, SMS, the clipboard, the microphone, the speaker, location data, Wi-Fi, base stations, and various sensors.     During screen lock or when apps reside in the background, the system restricts privacy permission requests based on strict security policies. This significantly lowers the risk of malicious apps acquiring sensitive data in the background without your knowledge, thereby reinforcing privacy protection during standby states.

    As shown in Figure 6-1, open the Device Management app on the Home Screen. In the Control Center panel below, tap the Cloud Space Management option. This module serves as the unified hub for managing remote storage nodes and automated backup strategies.

    As shown in Figure 6-5, accurately enter your cloud storage credentials on the setup interface:

                8.  How to Perform an Emergency Reset




    Designed for extreme and exceptional scenarios such as device loss or physical duress, VEILORA builds a three-tier cryptographic emergency system combined with rapid emergency response capabilities. This allows you to instantly elevate the device's security level, significantly mitigating the risk of core privacy data being unlawfully acquired.
    Standard Unlock Key (PIN): Used for routine device unlocking and seamlessly switching between dual-domain spaces, ensuring normal daily usage. Private Space Reset Key: Upon entering this key, the user appears to normally access the standard Main Space, while the system simultaneously and irreversibly wipes the Private Space back to its factory state. Device Reset Key: Triggers an immediate factory reset of the entire device, achieving the highest echelon of data protection.

     Once successfully connected, the interface will display the device's real-time (or last known) geographical location. As shown in Figure 8-19, tap Lost Mode in the function menu below.

     Enable Lost Mode: Toggle on the primary switch to prepare the remote device for locking.

     As shown in Figure 8-22, to prevent unauthorized operations, the system triggers an identity check. Enter the login password of your VEILORA OS management account in the prompted dialog box and tap OK.


⑦   Target Device Lock Screen Password Verification (Second-Tier Verification)

     To ensure the legitimacy and enforcement of the command, the system requires verification of the target device's low-level lock screen password, as shown in Figure 8-23:

     Dual-Space Verification: Enter the unlock PIN of the device in the Main Space Lock Screen Password field. If the device has an active Private Space, you must simultaneously enter the corresponding Private Space Lock Screen Password below.
